About Pinnacle

From its founding in 1929, the Pinnacle Hotel has had a long history of cultural, historical, and architectural integrity. When Alfonsino De Ratatouille started Pinnacle, he had one goal in mind: to provide the best genuine italian vacation experience to its customers. Ever since its humble beginnings back in 1929, the Pinnacle Hotel has truly become the pinnacle in the hotel business—with its unrivaled pools, sprawling rooms, and cozy suites, Pinnacle is the best in the west. Thousands flock from all over the world to have a chance to experience what people have called the “best hotel experience ever.”

Our Legacy

For generations, the De Rossi family worked as humble farmers in the italian suburbs. The great famine of 1923 deprived the fields of hydration, and the olive farmers of their harvests. determined to save his family's financial situation a few years later, Alfosino convinced his wife to turn their shed into a small hotel and bar for the locals. As word spread and the olive trees shriveled, Pinnacle became the primary attraction in the village. Months later, Ferrari automobiles lined the dirt path leading into the resort. Since then, the hotel has become an international attraction, sustained by the hands of later De Ratatouille generations.
